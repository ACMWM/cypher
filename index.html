    About Us

    Cypher VIII is the College of William & Mary's annual hackathon. We'll come together for a weekend and learn new stuff, have fun, and create cool projects. Come on down to learn from our workshops, get to know tech companies both local and nationwide, or compete for prizes! We pride ourselves on being accessible and welcoming to everyone, regardless of your skill or experience. So try something new! It's Cypher!

    Hybrid Hackathon

    This year, Cypher VIII is is fully in-person! Hackers will be able to join us at the Swem Library on the William & Mary campus.

    For more about our COVID-19 policy, see here.

    What Can I Make?

    New this year, we're branching out to all kinds of projects. Code or no code, if you can make it in a weekend, we want to see what you can do!

    Who's Invited?

    Cypher VIII is for anyone and everyone. Whether you've pioneered new machine learning techniques or you've never written a line of code in your life, we've got you covered. Cypher is for people of all interests and backgrounds, so if you've ever considered trying something new, now's your chance!
