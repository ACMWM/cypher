About Us

Cypher VI is the College of William and Mary's annual hackathon. It's an opportunity for students to come together for a weekend and learn new stuff, create cool projects, and win prizes. We pride ourselves on running an event that is accessible and welcoming to everyone, regardless of their skill or experience levels. We hope that Cypher VI will be a place for people to innovate and develop things that they had never considered before. This year our goal is to focus on sustainability and reducing the environmental impact of our hackathon.

Who's Invited?

Cypher VI is open to anyone and everyone. Whether you've pioneered new machine learning techniques or you don't know the difference between Python and Java, you're welcome at our hackathon. We want people of all interests and backgrounds to attend, so if you've ever even considered learning more about computer science, now is your time to take the dive.

Venue

Cypher VI will be held in Earl Gregg Swem Library. The main library on campus, Swem is home to 1,926,900 books, over 1,000,000 visitors per year, and one of the most beloved coffee shops on campus. Swem is a great place for collaboration, and we can't wait to fill it up with hackers. Look forward to lots of open tables, comfy couches, private rooms, and more! Check out the map here.

Hardware

Available hardware includes: Amazon Alexa Devices, Oculus Rifts, Arduinos, drones, 3D printers, and more!

Rules?

We want this to be a safe and fun event for everyone. All participants are expected to agree to the MLH Code of Conduct.

Travel Reimbursement?

Travel reimbursement requests are assessed on a case by case basis. A greater number of people traveling together (via carpooling, busing, etc.) will have a positive effect on the amount that we can reimburse.

We will calculate reimbursement primarily based on mileage. Those requesting reimbursement are asked to provide the number of people traveling with them to Cypher, the method of transportation and number of cars if applicable, and the estimated mileage for a one way trip. We will only fulfill reimbursements after receipts have been provided for each trip.

We will only cover necessary travel expenses such as gas, fares, etc. Meals will NOT be covered.

All requests should be submitted via email to cypher.wm@gmail.com. Please submit your requests ahead of time to guarantee that we can review them ahead of time.
